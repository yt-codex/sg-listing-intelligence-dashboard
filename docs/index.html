A weekly surveillance console for listing-side pressure, deterioration, easing, and unusual project / district signals.

How to read district signals

Pressure is the composite listing-side stress score for a district: more active inventory, price cuts, stale listings, and duplicate/shadow candidates push it up.

Deterioration means the district pressure score has risen versus the prior weekly snapshot — a worse listing-side signal, not a transaction-price forecast.

Easing means the district pressure score has fallen versus the prior weekly snapshot. Treat it as possible clearing or lower seller stress, not proof that the market has recovered.

How to read project signals

Pressure is the project/postal listing-side stress score: active inventory, price cuts, stale listings, top-agent concentration, and duplicate/shadow candidates.

Deterioration means a project/postal group’s pressure score rose week-on-week, so it may deserve inspection.

Easing means the score fell week-on-week. It is a lead for possible clearing, not a confirmed improvement in transacted prices.

Low confidence rows usually have small sample sizes or weaker project matching; use them as triage leads only.

Region pressure ranking

The mid-level read: broad enough to avoid project-level noise, but more actionable than a whole-market average. Use this as the first triage layer, then drill into districts or projects only where a region is moving.

Region score: 10% active-listing scale + 35% price-cut rate + 30% stale-60d share + 25% duplicate candidates.

District pressure ranking

A more granular seller-stress / listing-oversupply signal for the selected week, market, and segment. Use after the region table points to where movement is concentrated.

District score: 10% active-listing scale + 35% price-cut rate + 30% stale-60d share + 25% duplicate candidates.

Project / postal pressure ranking

Actual project rows are used where reliable. Title-only rows fall back to postal-code grouping where possible. Benchmark gaps compare project/postal pressure against the selected district.

Project/postal score: 10% active-listing scale + 35% price-cut rate + 30% stale-60d share + 10% top-agent share + 15% duplicate candidates.
